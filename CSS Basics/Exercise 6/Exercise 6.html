Shakshoka

Ingredients

  • 1 large chopped onion
  • 4 medium tomatoes, diced (preferably soft and ripe)
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, diced
  • 3–4 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 4 eggs

The process:

Heat a wide skillet with 1–2 tablespoons of oil and sauté the onion until lightly golden. Add the tomatoes, bell pepper, hot pepper, and garlic. Lightly salt, cover, and cook for a few minutes until the pepper softens slightly (stir occasionally).

Add the tomato paste and season with salt, black pepper, and paprika. Cook for a few more minutes, then add the eggs.

Cover and cook on low heat until the eggs reach your desired level of doneness.
